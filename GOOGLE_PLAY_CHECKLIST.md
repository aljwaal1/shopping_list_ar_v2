# Google Play Checklist - قائمة المشتريات

## App identity
- Developer: Explapp
- App name Arabic: قائمة المشتريات
- App package: com.explapp.shoppinglist
- First Play version: 1.0.0+1
- Category: Productivity or Tools

## Policy position
- The app works offline.
- The app does not require account login.
- The app does not request location, contacts, camera, microphone, SMS, or storage permissions.
- Shopping items are saved locally on the user's device using SharedPreferences.
- No ads are included in the first Google Play edition.
- No in-app purchases are included.

## Data Safety draft
- Data collected: No
- Data shared with third parties: No
- Data processed ephemerally: No
- Data encrypted in transit: Not applicable because the app does not send user data to a server.
- Users can delete local data inside the app by deleting items or clearing completed items.

- Confirm the AAB was created by GitHub Actions.

- Prepare at least 2 phone screenshots.
- Prepare 512x512 icon.
- Prepare feature graphic 1024x500.
- Use the privacy policy text in PRIVACY_POLICY.md or publish it on a public URL.
