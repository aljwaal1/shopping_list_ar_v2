# Privacy Policy - قائمة المشتريات

Last updated: 2026-07-01

Explapp built قائمة المشتريات as an offline shopping list application.

## Data collection
The app does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

## Local storage
Shopping list items, categories, quantities, and app settings are stored locally on the user's device. This information is not sent to Explapp or to any third party.

## Permissions
The app does not request sensitive permissions such as location, camera, microphone, contacts, SMS, or external storage.

## Internet and third parties
The first Google Play edition of the app does not use ads, analytics, external servers, or third-party tracking services.

## User control
Users can delete shopping items from inside the app. Users can also remove all local app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage from Android settings.

## Contact
For support or privacy questions, contact the developer using the email shown on the Google Play listing.
